St.Andrews Institute of Technology and Management (SAITM) Gurgaon is awarded ‘Best Engineering College for promoting Industry-Academic Interface’ by the Hon’ble Government of Goa Smt. Mridula Sinha. SAITM Gurgaon offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the discipline of Engineering, Management and Computer Application. Admission is done on the basis of merit and entrance examinations. The institute provides 100% placement assistance to its students. The highest package is recorded at INR 10 LPA and the average package is recorded at INR 4 LPA. Some of the potential employers include Amazon, Genpact, Tech Mahindra, Reliance, TATA, Dyson, BT, etc.

St. Andrews Institute of Technology and Management Admission

Online Mode

  1. Visit the official website of St. Andrews Institute and fill the application form and upload the required documents.
  2. Make a payment of the application fee of INR 500 either through Net Banking/ Credit/ Debit Card.
  3. Submit the duly filled form and save the same for future reference.

Offline Mode

  1. Visit the official website of St. Andrews Institute and download the application form.
  2. Fill the application form manually and pay the application fee of INR 500 in the form of a Demand Draft drawn in favor of St. Andrews Institute of Technology and Management.
